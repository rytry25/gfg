About

Oceans of Terra: Origins is a collection of 3600 subaquatic heroes that must defend the underwater world from evil forces. These heroes derive from 3 different species: Narwhals, Sea Turtles, and Mermaids. Together they must save the Oceans of Terra. Each PFP links to a playable character in the game.
